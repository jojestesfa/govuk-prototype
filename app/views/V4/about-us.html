About us

The National Careers Service offers you free, impartial advice and guidance to help make career decisions, achieve your goals and to develop the skills you need to find a job.

To make decisions about your future, it’s important to know what’s out there and what’s right for you. Whatever stage you are at in your career, we can help you understand what your skills are and what skills you might need to adapt to new jobs, circumstances and opportunities.

Learn more about how we can help you by watching our video.
