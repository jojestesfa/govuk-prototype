Your next steps

If you would like to change career but are unsure where to start, we recommend taking the following steps. If you already have a career in mind, see our advice on finding job vacancies.

{% include "includes/your-next-steps.html" %}

  1. Step 1 Skills assessment

    Take a skills assessment to think about the type of work you want to do. It takes 5 to 10 minutes. At the end, you’ll get a summary of job groups that might interest you based on your answers.

    Start assessment

  2. Step 2 Job profiles

    Use our job profiles to find out what experience or qualifications you need to enter the careers you are interested in.

    Search

  3. Step 3 Job market trends in your area

    Take a look at the labour market trends in your local area to understand the demand for the careers you are interested in.

    Search

  4. Step 4 Speak to an adviser

    Speak to one of our careers advisers to discuss your options for entering your chosen career(s).

    Start webchat

  5. Step 5 Job advice

    Once you are ready to search for a job and apply, see our advice on finding job vacancies.

    Find a job advice
