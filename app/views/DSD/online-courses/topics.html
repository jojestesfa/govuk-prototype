Online course subjects

Explore the following course subjects to find a suitable online course for you. Each subject offers courses at different qualification levels and concentrates on different skills.

If you are not sure which skills you want to develop or improve, you can take a skills assessment.

  • Improve your maths

    Learn basic maths skills, looking at how to work with numbers.

  • Improve your English

    Learn basic English skills like writing, grammar and spelling.

  • Computing, digital and IT

    Get started with basic computer skills, learn to use software like Word and Powerpoint.

  • Leadership and management

    Develop your communication skills and understand how to lead and motivate a team.

  • Finance, book-keeping and accounts

    Learn about managing finances and how to use accounting software.

  • Teaching, learning support and advice

    Gain practical skills to help you work with children and young adults.

  • Health and social care

    Learn how to care for people with mental and physical health conditions.

  • Business and administration

    Improve your workplace skills, like communication and performance.
