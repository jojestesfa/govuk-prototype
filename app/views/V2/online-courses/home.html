Online learning courses for furlough workers

If your employer puts you on temporary leave (furlough) because of coronavirus, you could be paid 80% of your wages while you’re off work. HM Revenue and Customs’ guidance can help you understand if you’re eligible.

You do not need to sign in or create an account to save your assessment. You can also complete it later and look at your results again at any time.

  • Find an online learning course
  • Find a job

If you have been put on furlough, you could take this opportunity to improve your skills or retrain by taking an online course.

The Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA) are offering online learning courses with their providers, and Microsoft and the Open University.

Find an online learning course

Learning courses are available in level 3 to 5 qualifications, in subject areas like:

  • Digital
  • Healthcare
  • Accounting
  • Social care
  • Business
  • Retail
