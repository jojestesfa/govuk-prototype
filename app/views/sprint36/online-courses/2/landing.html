Free digital courses online

Explore courses to help you improve your current digital skills or gain new ones.

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

The National Careers Service is working with the Open University to offer a range of free courses to help you improve your digital skills while you’re at home.

You can choose to study at the level that suits you, from introductory to advanced courses.

A digital skills course might be useful for you if you:

  • have been put on temporary leave (furlough) by your employer
  • are interested in learning new digital skills
  • want to develop the digital skills that you already have
  • want to move into a new career
