The benefits of online learning

There are many reasons online learning can benefit you. You can:

  • explore current or develop new interests
  • build on your skills or learn new ones
  • get on in your career with a new qualification
  • have fun, challenge yourself and use your spare time
  • fit it around your daily life
  • meet and share ideas with other learners online

Top tips for making online learning work for you

Follow our advice on making online learning work for you:

  • make sure you have all the materials and equipment you’ll need
  • check your your internet connection is working
  • find a quiet space to work
  • let everyone at home know the times you will be studying so there are no distractions
  • get help with childcare or other responsibilities if you can, so you can focus
  • make yourself a study plan
  • highlight any deadlines you’ll need to meet
  • ask a tutor for help if you need it
  • be kind to yourself - getting into studying online can take time and practice, so don’t give up
